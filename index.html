Image quality

Large images are downscaled when added, to keep the app and save files fast. Affects only images added afterwards. 3000 px ≈ 300 DPI across a full 3×3 spread; pick High or Original before adding images meant for even larger continuous spreads.

Slot size (pockets)

Marks a pocket as intentionally empty (Michi Method). Shows as a hatched pattern on canvas, stays clean in print. Press E to toggle.

Image crop (mm)

Wheel = zoom, drag = pan, arrows = 1mm (shift = 10mm), Ctrl+Z = undo.

Expand slot

Expand grows this slot by one pocket in the chosen direction; Trim removes the outermost pocket from that side, so expanding right and trimming right returns to where you started. Use Copy/Paste crop to place the same image in a separate pocket.

Print settings

Scale compensation for precise printing: print the calibration page, measure both 100 mm bars, and enter the measured lengths above. Michify counter-scales the output to cancel your printer's error. Leave at 100 for no compensation. Saved per browser, not per project.

About Michify

A precision printing tool for TCG binder art layouts. Design custom inserts, plan Michi Method spreads, and export print-ready layouts with millimeter-accurate dimensions.

Version: 4.2
License: MIT (free and open source)
Author: Risto Ruuskanen

The Michi Method was popularized by @peeplop on Instagram. Michify is an independent tool that supports designing for this method but is not officially affiliated with any Michi Method creators or communities.

This tool is free to use. If it saved you time, consider buying me a coffee to help maintain and improve it.
