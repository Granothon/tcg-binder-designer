About Michify

A precision printing tool for TCG binder art layouts. Design custom inserts, plan Michi Method spreads, and export print-ready layouts with millimeter-accurate dimensions.

Version: 3.1
License: MIT (free and open source)
Author: Risto Ruuskanen

The Michi Method was popularized by @peeplop on Instagram. Michify is an independent tool that supports designing for this method but is not officially affiliated with any Michi Method creators or communities.

This tool is free to use. If it saved you time, consider buying me a coffee to help maintain and improve it.
